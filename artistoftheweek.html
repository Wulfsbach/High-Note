Bio
Childish Gambino is the hip-hop alter ego of multi-hyphenate American entertainer Donald Glover, who is also known for acting roles on television shows like Community and the Emmy- and Golden Globe-winning Atlanta, as well as the film Solo: A Star Wars Story. As Childish Gambino, Glover performs off-kilter hip-hop and R&B-inspired funk, for which he won a Grammy for Best Traditional R&B Performance in 2017 for his single, "Redbone."

In 2018, his politically-charged song "This Is America" debuted at number one atop the Billboard Hot 100, buoyed by a viral music video that addressed gun violence and racial injustice.
